About Us

| About Us

Best Club & In Town

Welcome to Club Ninety Limited! Established in 2023, we are a vibrant social and recreational sanctuary dedicated to the SSC graduates of 1990 from Bangladesh. Our primary goal is to nurture connections, enhance well-being, and craft unforgettable experiences through a wide array of events and services tailored for our members and their families.

At Club Ninety, we pride ourselves on offering modern amenities that cater to diverse interests. Our facilities include top-notch sports complexes, engaging recreational programs, and exclusive venues designed for memorable gatherings. Our activities span from athletic competitions and cultural celebrations to support initiatives, all aimed at promoting the essence of lifelong friendship and collaboration. We believe that together, we can cultivate a community steeped in mutual support, shared experiences, and lasting memories.

| Our Mission

Best Club & In Town

To cultivate an inclusive environment where members and families can enjoy recreational, cultural, and educational opportunities, fostering strong social bonds and personal growth. We aim to support the well-being of our members by providing an enriching space for all to connect, collaborate, and thrive. Through diverse programs and services, we foster personal development, social engagement, and community support. At Club Ninety, we believe in strengthening the ties that bind us and contributing positively to each other’s lives, creating a legacy of shared experiences and mutual respect.
| Our Vission

Best Club In Town

To be the premier social club for SSC-1990 graduates of Bangladesh, promoting unity, support, and vibrant lifestyles for our members and future generations. We envision an empowered community where lasting friendships are formed, and each member feels valued, supported, and connected. By fostering lifelong learning, cultural celebration, and a spirit of camaraderie, we aim to create a hub where alumni and their families can grow together. We aspire to set a benchmark in community building and member satisfaction, impacting future generations with a legacy of unity and support.
