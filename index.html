| About Us

Best Club In Town

Club Ninety Limited, founded in 2023, is a social and recreational haven designed for the 1990 SSC graduates of Bangladesh. Our mission is to foster connections, promote well-being, and create memorable experiences through diverse events and services. We offer state-of-the-art amenities, including sports facilities, recreational programs, and exclusive venues for members and their families. With activities ranging from sports to cultural gatherings and support initiatives, Club Ninety champions the spirit of lifelong camaraderie. Together, we continue to shape a community grounded in friendship, support, and shared experiences, enriching lives through meaningful social engagement.
