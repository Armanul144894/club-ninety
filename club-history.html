Club History

| Our History

Club Ninety Limited was founded in 2023 as a limited guarantee company to create a supportive community for the 1990 SSC alumni. Our mission centers around fostering lifelong bonds and promoting a collaborative spirit. Initially envisioned as a social platform, Club Ninety has since evolved into a comprehensive hub with amenities and activities ranging from sports and cultural gatherings to family-friendly entertainment.
We strive to offer unparalleled experiences, celebrating alumni achievements and supporting individual growth. Our club embodies a pioneering vision in alumni engagement, inspiring unity through social events, networking, and development programs.
